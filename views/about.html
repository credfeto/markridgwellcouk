About Me

I am passionate about developing high quality software that is simple and easy to use. I enjoy taking photos and from this work on the Diffractor project which aims to help make sense of and organize a photo library. Enabling you to find and share the events of the past before they get lost in memory.
I often take photos of local events like 'The Annual Boxing-Day Tug of War at Moreton, Essex'.
